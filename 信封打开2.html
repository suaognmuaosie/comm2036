Letter to Husband

Dear Galen,

I hope you are doing well. During this time away from Earth, I think of you constantly. The sights on Mars are truly magnificent, but they also make me miss our home on Earth, and every warm moment we shared together.

As an engineer exploring Mars colonization, I work hard every day, hoping to pave the way for a new home for humanity. However, every night when darkness falls and I look at the Martian sky, I can't help but think of the nights we spent stargazing together. Your support and understanding are my greatest motivation. I know you are taking on great responsibilities back on Earth, taking care of our home.

I miss your laughter, your embrace, and every ordinary yet beautiful moment we spent together. Although we are thousands of miles apart now, I want you to know that my heart is always with you. I can't wait to return to you once my mission is complete and share this extraordinary experience with you.

Love, Zara
