Letter to Mom

Dear Mom,

How have you been lately? My work on Mars is progressing smoothly, but I miss you very much. The scenery here, though magnificent, is nothing compared to the place you hold in my heart. Whenever I face difficulties and challenges, I always think of the resilience and courage you taught me.

Everything on Mars is new and fascinating, but it is also full of unknowns and challenges. As a Mars colonization engineer, I work hard every day, striving to create a new living space for humanity. Your support and love have always been my greatest motivation, allowing me to feel warmth even on this unfamiliar land.

I remember you always telling me when I was young that the world is vast and I should be brave to explore. Now, I have truly come to another planet, and none of this would have been possible without your guidance and encouragement. I hope that one day, you can see the beautiful sights of Mars with your own eyes, and we can share this extraordinary experience together.

Please take good care of yourself. I will complete my mission as soon as possible and return to you. When I come back, we will have much to talk about and many stories to share. I love you, Mom. Thank you for your constant support and love.

Forever loving you, Your daughter Zara
